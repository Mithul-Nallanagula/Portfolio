This is my latest workout plan


I hope u like my Workout plan:

Monday

The Monday workout targets the chest and triceps, focusing on exercises like bench press, incline dumbbell press, and triceps dips. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, emphasizing proper form. This routine builds upper body strength and muscle definition efficiently.

 Tuesday

The Tuesday workout focuses on the back and biceps, with exercises like pull-ups, bent-over rows, and barbell curls. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, ensuring proper form and muscle engagement. This routine helps build a strong back and well-defined biceps.

 Wednesday

The Wednesday workout targets legs and shoulders with exercises like squats, lunges, overhead presses, and lateral raises. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, maintaining proper form throughout. This routine strengthens the lower body and builds shoulder definition.

 Thursday(Repeatation of Monday)

The Thursday workout mirrors Monday, focusing on chest and triceps with exercises like bench press, incline dumbbell press, and triceps dips. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps, ensuring proper form and resting 60-90 seconds between sets. This routine continues to build upper body strength and muscle definition.

 Friday(Repeatation of Tuesday)

The Friday workout mirrors Tuesday, focusing on the back and biceps with exercises like pull-ups, bent-over rows, and barbell curls. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps, emphasizing proper form and allowing 60-90 seconds of rest between sets. This routine strengthens the back and sculpts the biceps effectively.

 Sadurday(Repeatation of wednesday)

The Saturday workout mirrors Wednesday, targeting legs and shoulders with exercises like squats, lunges, overhead presses, and lateral raises. Perform 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps with proper form, resting 60-90 seconds between sets. This routine builds lower body strength and shoulder definition.
